About Me

Introduction: Graduated from the University of Oregon with a double major in Humanities and Psychology, with a minor in English. After graduation, I moved to Osaka, Japan to teach English and travel. I spent four years abroad, returning to my hometown of Portland, Oregon in order to persue a career in the technology field. The problem-solving and 'Eureka!' nature of coding is what most appeals to me.
